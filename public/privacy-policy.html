Privacy Policy for FocusTube

Effective Date: June 2, 2026

FocusTube ("we," "our," or "us") is dedicated to helping you stay focused by minimizing distractions on YouTube. We believe your data belongs to you, and we have built this extension with privacy as a core principle.

1. Data Collection and Storage

FocusTube does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data.

  • All focus session data (history, duration, statistics) is stored locally on your device using Chrome's Local Storage API.
  • We do not send your data to any remote servers, cloud databases, or third-party analytics services.

2. Browsing History

We do not track your general browser history. FocusTube only runs on youtube.com and processes the metadata (such as video titles, channel names, and accessible labels) of YouTube elements dynamically on your device to determine what should be hidden based on your active focus keywords.

3. Keyword Processing

Any topics or keywords you enter to filter content are processed and stored entirely within the extension on your device. They are not transmitted to or analyzed by any external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

FocusTube does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since we do not collect your information, we cannot notify you individually of changes, but any updates will be reflected in this document with a new "Effective Date".

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support mechanisms.
